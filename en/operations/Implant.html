What is Dental Implantology?

Dental implantology is a field of dentistry focused on the use of dental implants to replace missing teeth. These implants are surgically placed into the jawbone, where they provide a permanent base for fixed, replacement teeth. Unlike dentures, dental implants are designed to look, feel, and function like your natural teeth and are a long-term solution for people suffering from missing teeth, failing teeth, or chronic dental problems.
